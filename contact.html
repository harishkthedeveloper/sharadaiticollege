Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Students should have passed 10th (SSC) examination from any recognized board and should be at least 14 years old.

What is the course duration?

All our programs are 2-year courses that combine theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on training.

Is there a placement guarantee?

We have a 95% placement record. Our dedicated placement cell provides comprehensive support to all students.

Do you offer scholarship programs?

Yes, we offer various scholarship schemes based on merit and financial need. Contact our office for details.

What programs are available?

We offer Electronics, Mechanical, and Information Technology programs. Visit our Programs page for more details.

How do I apply?

You can apply online through our website or visit our campus directly. Contact us for detailed application procedures.

About Our College

Know more about Sharada Pvt ITI College

Who We Are

Sharada Pvt ITI College is a premier institution dedicated to providing high-quality technical and vocational education. Located in Palakendram, Thorur, Telangana, we are committed to developing skilled professionals who can meet the demands of the ever-evolving industrial and technological landscape.

Our Mission

To impart quality technical education that empowers students with practical skills, knowledge, and values necessary for personal growth and meaningful contribution to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading technical institution recognized for excellence in education, research, and industry collaboration, producing professionals who are globally competitive and socially responsible.

Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our endeavors and maintain highest standards in education and training.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in our relationships and professional conduct.

Innovation

We embrace innovation and encourage creative thinking among our faculty and students.

Inclusivity

We provide equal opportunities for all students regardless of background or circumstances.

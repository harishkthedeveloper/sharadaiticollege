Our Programs

Explore our comprehensive range of technical courses

We offer industry-aligned programs designed to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge required in today's competitive market.

Electronics

Course Duration: 2 Years

Overview: Master the fundamentals of electronics and electrical systems. This program covers circuit design, troubleshooting, and maintenance of electronic equipment.

Key Subjects:

  • Basic Electronics and Semiconductor Devices
  • Circuit Analysis and Design
  • Digital Electronics
  • Microprocessor and Microcontroller
  • Power Supply Systems
  • Industrial Electronics

Career Opportunities:

  • Electronics Technician
  • Maintenance Supervisor
  • Quality Control Inspector
  • Field Service Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Developer

Mechanical
